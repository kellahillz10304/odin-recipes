Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is an antipasto dish, from Italy, consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt.
Variations may include toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, or cheese. In Italy, bruschetta is often
prepared using a brustolina grill.

Ingredients

  • Grilled bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Toast bread in oven or toaster oven
  2. Chop tomatoes
  3. Drizzle tomatoes with olive oil
  4. Mince garlic and add to tomatoes
  5. Cut basil and add to tomatoes
  6. Scoop tomatoes, garlic, and basil onto toasted bread pieces
  7. Enjoy!
