Crusty White Bread

Description

The most basic of all no-knead loaves, this is a wonderful
way to get into yeast-bread baking. The easy stir-together
dough rests in your refrigerator, developing flavor all the time,
till you're ready to bake. About 90 minutes before you want to serve bread,
grab a handful of dough, shape it, let it rise, then bake for 30 minutes.
The result? Incredible, crusty artisan-style bread, worthy of its 2016 Recipe of the Year title.

Ingredients

  • 7 1/2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons instant yeast

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  2. Mix everyting together
  3. Place dough in refrigerator for 4 hours
  4. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  5. Take dough out of refrigerator
  6. Cover hands in olive oil and form the dough into a ball
  7. Let dough rest for 45 minutes on a piece of parchment paper
  8. Transfer dough on parchment paper to baking stone in oven
  9. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes
  10. Take out of the oven and cover with a cloth
  11. Allow bread to rest for 1 hour
  12. Enjoy!
